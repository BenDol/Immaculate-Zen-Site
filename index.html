About

Immaculate Zen is an independent music project producing instrumental lo-fi, chillhop, ambient, and focus-oriented beats. The sound sits between late-night study sessions and quiet reflection — jazzy chord progressions, vinyl textures, soft drums, and atmospheric layering designed to fade into the background while you work, read, drift, or lock in.

Every release is self-produced, self-mixed, and self-published. Releases are distributed primarily through YouTube, with selected tracks published to other social platforms to help listeners discover the catalog.

Publishing Integration

Immaculate Zen uses a self-built publishing tool to release tracks and short-form videos from the project's catalog to connected social accounts (YouTube, TikTok, X, and others). The tool publishes content only from the Immaculate Zen catalog, only when the operator explicitly clicks "Publish," and only to accounts that have been connected via each platform's official OAuth flow.

The tool does not read other users' content, does not automate engagement (likes, follows, comments), and does not share data with third parties. See the Privacy Policy for full details on what data is collected and how it is stored, and the Terms of Service for use restrictions.
