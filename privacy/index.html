Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Immaculate Zen publishing tool (the "App") collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with the operation of the Immaculate Zen independent music project. The App is used internally by the Immaculate Zen operator to publish original music content to connected social media platforms.

1. Who We Are

Immaculate Zen is an independent music project. The App is a private tool used by the project's operator to manage releases and publish original music content to social platforms (YouTube, TikTok, X/Twitter, Meta platforms, and others) through each platform's official API.

2. Scope of This Policy

This policy covers the App's handling of data obtained through social platform APIs. Actions you take on the destination platforms themselves (YouTube, TikTok, etc.) are governed by those platforms' own privacy policies and terms.

3. What Data We Collect

When a social account is connected to the App via OAuth, we collect and store only the minimum information required to publish content on the connected account's behalf:

Data Purpose
OAuth access token and refresh token To call the platform API on the connected account's behalf when publishing a post or video, and to renew expired access tokens without re-prompting the operator.
Connected account identifier (open ID or equivalent) To associate the stored tokens with the correct account and prevent posting to the wrong account.
Display name and avatar URL Shown in the App's internal "Accounts" page so the operator can visually confirm which account is connected before publishing.
Published post / video ID and URL returned by the platform Stored so the App can link back to the published content for internal tracking.

4. Platform-Specific Data (TikTok)

When the TikTok integration is connected, the App requests the following TikTok scopes through the official Login Kit / OAuth 2.0 flow (PKCE):

  • user.info.basic - to retrieve the connected account's open ID, display name, and avatar for internal confirmation of the connected account.
  • video.upload - to upload a finished music video (MP4) generated inside the App from Immaculate Zen's own audio and artwork.
  • video.publish - to publish the uploaded video to the connected account's profile via TikTok's Content Posting API (Direct Post), with a caption and privacy level chosen by the operator per post.

The App does not read the connected account's videos, followers, likes, comments, inbox, feed, or any other content. It does not read or interact with any other TikTok user's data.

5. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information from end viewers of the published content.
  • We do not read or store timelines, feeds, followers, or other users' content.
  • We do not collect payment, financial, or device identifier information.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising SDKs inside the App.
  • We do not perform any scraping, aggregation, or data mining on any platform.

6. How We Use the Data

Collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Publish Immaculate Zen original music content to connected accounts when the operator explicitly initiates a publish action.
  • Display the correctly connected account in the App's internal UI.
  • Refresh expired access tokens silently so the operator does not need to re-authorize on every session.
  • Surface success/failure information for each publish attempt.

7. How Data Is Stored

All OAuth tokens are stored in a local, private database accessible only to the App's operator. Tokens are encrypted at rest. The database is not exposed to the public internet and is not shared with any third parties. No copies of the database are distributed.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties. Data is transmitted only between the App and the official API endpoints of the connected platforms (such as open.tiktokapis.com, api.twitter.com, googleapis.com) over HTTPS, exclusively for the purposes described in this policy.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Tokens and associated metadata are retained only while an account remains connected. The App's operator can disconnect any connected account at any time from the App's Accounts page, which immediately deletes the stored access token, refresh token, and associated display name/avatar for that account.

You may also revoke the App's access directly from the connected platform's own security settings:

  • TikTok: Settings → Manage app permissions
  • YouTube / Google: Google Account → Third-party apps with account access
  • X / Twitter: Settings → Connected apps

10. Children's Privacy

The App is an internal publishing tool and is not directed to children. It does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

11. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored data, including encrypting tokens at rest, transmitting all API calls over HTTPS, and running the App only in a private environment controlled by the operator. However, no system is perfectly secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

13. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact the Immaculate Zen project via the YouTube channel.
